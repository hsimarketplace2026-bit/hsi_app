HSI is made possible by the generosity and commitment of these organisations working together for the Bintulu community.

Main Sponsor

PTT Exploration & Production (PTTEP)

With a deep commitment to social responsibility and community empowerment, PTTEP provides the financial and strategic support that drives the programme.

Collaborator & Land Contributor

Bintulu Development Authority (BDA)

BDA plays a vital role as collaborator and land contributor, making the necessary land available for the project to take root.

Financial Steward & Implementer

Steward of the funds and resources, ensuring they are allocated effectively and aligned with the goals and objectives of the programme.
