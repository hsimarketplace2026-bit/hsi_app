The Journey

HSI Programme Activities

A three-year community development program empowering B40 and low-income households in Bintulu through hydroponic farming and entrepreneurship — a collaboration between PTTEP, BDA and the Lions Club of Bintulu Kidurong.

1

Technical Training

Classroom and practical training from hydroponic experts — system setup, nutrient management, environmental control, cultivation and maintenance.

2

Hands-On Farming

Participants manage their own hydroponic plots — planting, monitoring growth, maintaining systems and solving real-world challenges under guidance.

3

Business Development

Training in business planning, cost management, branding, packaging, market analysis and sales — supported by an online marketing platform.

4

Harvest & Sales

Participants cultivate, harvest, market and sell their produce — gaining experience across the full farm-to-market value chain while earning income.

5

Mentorship & Graduation

Outstanding graduates become mentors for new participants, receive starter kits, and selected mentors get support to set up commercial farms.
