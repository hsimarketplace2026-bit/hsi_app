About the Program

The Hydro Sprout Initiative

A groundbreaking hydroponic community development program in Bintulu — fostering sustainable agriculture, improving food security, and empowering local communities through modern farming.

Driven by a multi-stakeholder partnership — PTTEP as main sponsor, the Bintulu Development Authority as land contributor, Universiti Putra Malaysia providing technical expertise, and the Lions Club of Bintulu Kidurong as financial steward — HSI leverages hydroponic technology to address local food-supply challenges, create entrepreneurship opportunities, and uplift low-income communities.

What is Hydroponic Farming?

Growing more, with far less.

Hydroponics grows plants without soil, delivering nutrient-rich water directly to the roots in a controlled environment — enabling faster growth, higher yields, and year-round cultivation while using up to 90% less water, less land, and minimal pesticides.

💧

Saves Water

Up to 90% less than soil farming via closed-loop recirculation.

🚫

No Pesticides

Clean, controlled conditions keep produce naturally pesticide-free.

📅

Year-Round Harvest

Climate-controlled growing means fresh produce every season.

⚡

Faster Growth

Roots get nutrients directly, so plants grow up to twice as fast.

A 3-Year Journey

HSI Activities

From classroom to market — empowering B40 and low-income households through hydroponic farming and entrepreneurship.

1

Technical Training

Classroom and practical training from hydroponic experts — system setup, nutrient management, environmental control and farm maintenance.

2

Hands-On Farming

Participants manage hydroponic plots — planting, monitoring growth, maintaining systems and solving real-world challenges under guidance.

3

Business Development

Training in business planning, cost management, branding, packaging and sales — supported by this online marketplace platform.

4

Harvest & Sales

Participants cultivate, harvest, market and sell their produce — gaining experience across the complete farm-to-market value chain.

5

Mentorship & Graduation

Outstanding graduates become mentors, with starter kits and support to establish their own commercial hydroponic farms.

The Outcome

Improved livelihoods, stronger food security, new jobs, and a new generation of sustainable agropreneurs in Bintulu.
