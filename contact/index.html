Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How do I become a seller on HSI Marketplace?

Sign up via the "Login / Register" button and choose "I'm a Seller". Our admin team reviews farmer applications within 2–3 business days.

Can I visit the hydroponic farm?

Yes! We host farm tours every Saturday morning. Please use the form above to request a slot at least 3 days in advance.

Do you ship outside Bintulu?

Each seller sets their own delivery range. Most farmers deliver within Bintulu and nearby townships. Check individual seller profiles for their delivery coverage.

How can my organisation sponsor the programme?

We welcome corporate sponsorships and CSR partnerships. Reach out via partnerships@hsi-marketplace.org and we'll set up a meeting.
